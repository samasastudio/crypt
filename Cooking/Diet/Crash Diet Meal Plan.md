# TJ Miracle Diet — Weekly Meal Itinerary
**Sam & Leslie | Week of Feb 23, 2026**

---

## Daily Anchors
- **TJ Soup** (make full batch Monday): Large onion, 2–3 green peppers, head of cabbage, large stalk of celery, tomatoes, 2 pkgs Lipton Onion Soup Mix. Boil 10 min, simmer until soft.
- **V8 Veggie Soup** (alternate days): 2 cans V8 + chopped mushrooms, celery, cabbage, onion, green pepper. Simmer 5–8 min. Season with garlic powder + Bolner's.
- Soup rotation: **TJ Soup** Mon/Wed/Sat, **V8 Veggie Soup** Tue/Fri/Sun
