# Bachans Chicken & Veggie Bowl

**Air Fryer + Rice Cooker** | Serves 2 | 25 min

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 3 tbsp Bachan's (Hot & Spicy or Yuzu)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 bunch broccolini, trimmed
- 1 bell pepper, sliced (or mini peppers)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 1 cup basmati rice (dried)
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Extra Bachan's for drizzle

## Steps

1. Start rice cooker (1 cup rice + 1.5 cups water)
2. Cut chicken into 1" cubes, toss with Bachan's + soy sauce
3. Trim broccolini, slice peppers, toss with olive oil, salt & pepper
4. **Basket 1:** Chicken at 380°F for 14 min — shake at 4 & 8 min, brush extra Bachan's at 8 min
5. **Basket 2:** Broccolini + peppers at 380°F for 10 min — add at 4:00 mark, shake at 8 min
6. Check chicken internal temp: 165°F
7. Divide rice between bowls, arrange veggies + glazed chicken
8. Drizzle extra Bachan's, top with sesame seeds & green onion

## Notes

- Chicken starts first — Bachan's needs time to caramelize
- Second Bachan's brush at 8 min builds that sticky glaze
- Hot & Spicy brings heat; Yuzu adds citrus brightness
- Thin-sliced chicken breast works great — reduce cook to 10 min
- Swap broccolini for asparagus or snap peas if preferred
