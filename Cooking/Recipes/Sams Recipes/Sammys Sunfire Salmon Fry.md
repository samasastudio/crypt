# Sammys Sunfire Salmon Fry

**Air Fryer (Dual Basket)** | Serves 2 | 30 min

## Ingredients

- 1 lb sweet potatoes, cubed
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1 lb salmon fillets
- 2 tbsp Bachan's sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Cooking spray
- 1 cup jasmine rice (dried)
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 2 sheets seaweed, torn

## Steps

1. Start rice cooker (1 cup rice + 1.5 cups water)
2. Cut sweet potatoes into 1" cubes, mix Bachan's + soy sauce
3. **Basket 1:** Sweet potatoes at 400°F for 18 min with salt, pepper, cinnamon
4. **Basket 2:** At 8:00 mark, spray basket, brush salmon with Bachan's mix — 400°F for 10 min
5. Shake at 9:00 (sync pauses both baskets)
6. Both done at 18:00 — salmon at 145°F internal
7. Divide rice, add salmon, sweet potatoes, cucumber, avocado, seaweed

## Notes

- Sweet potatoes take longer — staggered start syncs finish times
- No foil needed — spray basket for easy cleanup
- Cinnamon adds unexpected warmth to sweet potatoes
- Bachan's glaze caramelizes perfectly on basket surface
