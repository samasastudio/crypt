# Loaded Turkey Taco Bowls

**Air Fryer + Rice Cooker** | Serves 2-3 | 25 min

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground turkey (93% lean)
- 1 packet Siete Spicy Taco Seasoning
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1/2 yellow onion, diced
- 4 oz white mushrooms, sliced (optional)
- 1 tbsp Bolner's Fajita Seasoning
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 cup basmati rice (dried)
- 1 can H-E-B Organics Fat Free Refried Black Beans
- 2 cups romaine, chopped
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese
- 2-3 tbsp sour cream
- Cholula to taste

## Steps

1. Start rice cooker (1 cup rice + 1.5 cups water)
2. Season turkey with taco seasoning + garlic, mix well
3. Toss diced veggies with olive oil + fajita seasoning
4. **Basket 1:** Turkey at 375°F for 10-12 min — break apart at 4 & 7 min
5. **Basket 2:** Mushrooms at 375°F from start; add peppers + onions at 4:00
6. Check turkey internal: 165°F, then mix turkey + veggies together
7. Divide rice, warm beans alongside, add romaine, spoon turkey-veggie mix
8. Top with cheese, avocado, sour cream, Cholula

## Notes

- Turkey releases liquid — drain halfway if pooling
- Garlic in the turkey adds depth; Siete Spicy brings the heat
- Bolner's Fajita on the veggies gives that Tex-Mex char flavor
- Beans add protein and fiber; warm in microwave or small pot
- Serves 3 lighter portions or 2 hearty bowls
