# Turkey Mushroom Vodka Pasta

**Air Fryer + Stovetop** | Serves 2-3 | 14 min

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground turkey
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 8 oz Banza chickpea penne
- 1 jar Rao's vodka sauce
- Italian seasoning
- Salt & pepper
- Olive oil

## Steps

1. Season ground turkey with Italian seasoning, salt, pepper
2. Slice mushrooms, toss with olive oil + Italian seasoning
3. Start boiling water
4. **Basket 1:** Turkey at 375°F for 10 min — break apart at 4 min
5. **Basket 2:** Mushrooms at 375°F for 8 min starting at 2:00 — shake halfway
6. Drop pasta at 4:00, drain at 10:00
7. Combine turkey + mushrooms + sauce with pasta, let meld 2 min

## Notes

- Philips dual basket sync feature auto-aligns finish times
- Turkey releases liquid — drain halfway for better browning
- Mushrooms shrink significantly, fill basket generously
- Chickpea pasta cooks faster than traditional — watch timing
