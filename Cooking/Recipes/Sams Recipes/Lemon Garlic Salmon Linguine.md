# Lemon Garlic Salmon Linguine

**Air Fryer + Stovetop** | Serves 2 | 20 min

## Ingredients

- 1 lb salmon fillet
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
- 8 oz white mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tbsp Bolner's Lemon Herb
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- 8 oz Banza chickpea linguine
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, zested + juiced
- 1/4 cup reserved pasta water
- 1/4 cup parmesan, grated

## Steps

1. Start boiling salted water, season salmon with Bolner's Lemon Herb
2. **Basket 2:** Mushrooms at 375°F for 12 min (start first)
3. **Basket 1:** Salmon at 380°F for 10 min, starting at 2:00, flip at 8:00
4. Drop linguine at 2:00, drain at 12:00 (reserve 1/4 cup water)
5. Add asparagus to mushroom basket at 4:00 mark
6. Salmon done at 145°F, rest briefly
7. Stovetop: butter + garlic 30 sec, toss pasta + veggies + lemon + parm

## Notes

- Mushrooms start first — they shrink significantly and need extra time
- Banza linguine cooks faster than wheat — taste at 9 min for al dente
- Pasta water is KEY — add more if sauce gets tight
- Toss veggies into pasta for final mix — they soak up the lemon butter
