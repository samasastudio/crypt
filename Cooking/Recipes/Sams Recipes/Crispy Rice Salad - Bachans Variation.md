# Crispy Rice Salad - Bachans Variation

**Air Fryer (Dual Basket)** | Serves 2 | 35 min

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked jasmine rice, cooled
- 2 tbsp Bachan's Hot & Spicy
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 3 tbsp Bachan's Hot & Spicy (for chicken)
- 1-2 cucumbers, sliced
- 3 green onions, sliced
- 1 avocado, chopped
- 1 cup edamame
- Sesame seeds
- 1/4 cup H-E-B Light Sour Cream
- 2 tbsp light mayo
- 3 tbsp A Taste of Thai Sweet Chili
- Squeeze of lime

## Steps

1. **Basket 1:** Rice + Bachan's + sesame oil at 375°F, shake every 5 min (18 min total)
2. **Basket 2:** Chicken cubes + Bachan's at 380°F starting at 5:00 mark, brush more at 10 min
3. Whisk dressing: sour cream, mayo, sweet chili, lime
4. Combine cucumber, edamame, green onion, avocado
5. Add crispy rice and glazed chicken, drizzle dressing, toss gently

## Notes

- Bachan's on the rice creates umami-rich crispy bits
- Rice starts first — needs more time to get truly crispy
- Sub Impossible Nuggies for plant-based version
- HEB carries A Taste of Thai Sweet Red Chili Sauce in Asian aisle
