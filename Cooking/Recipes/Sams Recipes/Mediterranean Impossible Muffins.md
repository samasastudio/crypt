# Mediterranean Impossible Muffins

**Air Fryer Only** | Serves 2-3 | 14 min

## Ingredients

- 8 oz Impossible ground
- 4 English muffins, split
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Cholula or Yellowbird Habanero
- Fresh lemon juice
- Italian seasoning
- Salt & pepper
- Avocado oil spray

## Steps

1. Form Impossible into 4 thin patties, season with Italian seasoning + salt + pepper
2. Toss mushrooms with oil spray + Italian seasoning
3. Spray muffin cut sides with avocado oil
4. **Basket 1:** Patties + mushrooms at 380°F for 8 min — flip patties at 4 min
5. **Basket 2:** Muffins at 380°F starting at 4:00 — flip at 6 min, done at 7 min
6. Stack: muffin bottom, patty, goat cheese, mushrooms, avocado, lemon, hot sauce, top

## Notes

- Impossible cooks faster than beef — don't overdo it or it dries out
- Smash patties thin for better muffin-to-meat ratio
- Goat cheese doesn't melt like pepper jack — it softens and spreads
- Lemon + goat cheese + avocado is the magic combo here
- Italian seasoning ties the earthy mushrooms to the tang
- Open-faced works well if the stack gets too tall
