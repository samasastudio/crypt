# Crispy Rice Salad

**Air Fryer + Stovetop** | Serves 2 | 45 min

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked jasmine rice, cooled
- 1.5 tsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp chilli crisp oil
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1-2 cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 1 cup edamame, shelled
- 3 green onions, sliced
- 1 avocado, chopped
- Crispy chicken tenders
- Chopped peanuts (optional)
- 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt
- 1/4 cup light mayo
- 3 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
- 1/2 tsp paprika

## Steps

1. Toss cooled rice with soy sauce, chilli crisp oil, and sesame oil
2. Air fry rice at 375°F for 15-20 min, shaking every 5 min until golden
3. Cook chicken tenders at 400°F for 10 min, flip halfway, slice into strips
4. Whisk yoghurt, mayo, sweet chilli sauce, and paprika for dressing
5. Combine cucumber, edamame, green onion, avocado in large bowl
6. Add crispy rice and sliced chicken, drizzle dressing, toss gently
7. Garnish with sesame seeds and peanuts
