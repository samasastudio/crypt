# Vegetarian lasagna

**Pasta** | Page 108 | Serves 5 | 468 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 468 | 30.4g | 36.6g | 20.7g |

## Ingredients

- 300g dried pasta
- 5-6 Italian pork sausages
- 5 tbs tomato paste (80g)
- 250g light thickened
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1/4-1/3 cup parmesan

## Notes

We use Vetta Smart Protein Pasta, Coles Finest Australian Pork Sausages (Italian Style) and Philadelphia 60% Less Fat Light Cooking Cream for these macros. For a lower calorie and lower fat option, replace cooking cream with plain Greek yoghurt.
