# Tomato burst parpadelle

|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 400-500g cherry tomatoes
- 2 sweet-pointed
- 1 block light cream cheese
- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves the pasta per packet directions
- 1/4 tsp each - dried
- Pinch of chilli flakes
- 1-2 cups spinach well
- 300g dried pasta

## Notes

We use Vetta Smart Protein Pasta and Philadelphia 40% Less Fat Light Spreadable Cream Cheese for these macros. To make the pasta extra yum, use a sweet chilli cream cheese. The sauce is generous if you wish to add more pasta. Optional to add chicken for extra protein.
