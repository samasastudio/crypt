# 4-ingredient spaghetti

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 4- ingredient
- 350g dried pasta
- 60g light cream cheese
- 420g canned tomato soup

## Notes

Cream cheese can be substituted with milk. Optional to add in extra veggies or protein of choice (bacon, chicken etc). We use Heinz Big Red Salt Reduced Condensed Tomato Soup, Vetta Protein Pasta, Philadelphia 40% Less Fat Light Spreadable Cream Cheese and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros.
