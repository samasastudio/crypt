# Pesto pasta bake

**Pasta** | Page 86 | Serves 4 | 477 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 477 | 47.5g | 33.8g | 13.7g |

## Ingredients

- 200g dried penne pasta
- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 1/2 onion
- 1 tsp garlic
- 300g extra lean beef
- 2 cups jarred pasta sauce
- 1 egg
- 1 cup low fat cottage a bowl
- 1 cup light grated cheese
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning Cover with foil

## Notes

We use Vetta Smart Protein Penne Pasta and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros. Optional to add extra vegetables, we recommend grated carrot or zucchini.
