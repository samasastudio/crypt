# Creamy Nando's chicken pasta

**Pasta** | Page 94 | Serves 4 | 473 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 473 | 49.6g | 37.0g | 10.4g |

## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast
- 1-2 tbs Cajun/ Fajita
- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 1 red onion, diced
- 2 x red capsicums, diced
- 240g dried pasta
- 1 tbs fresh parsley browned
- 70g tomato
- 350ml chicken stock
- 150g light cream cheese and pasta to the pan, mix
- 30g cheese, grated

## Notes

We use Vetta Smart Protein pasta, Philadelphia 40% Less Fat Light Spreadable Cream Cheese and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros.
