# Creamy red pasta

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast, diced
- 1 tbs peri-peri/ all-
- 1 red onion, diced
- 2 x capsicums (red/ season with paprika and oregano
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 200g dried pasta
- 70g tomato
- 60g Nando’s sauce
- 350ml chicken stock
- 150g light cream cheese

## Notes

We use Vetta Smart Protein Pasta and Nando’s Medium or Mild Peri-Peri Chilli Sauce for these macros. You can substitute the peri seasoning with 1 tsp each paprika, coriander, onion powder and garlic powder.
