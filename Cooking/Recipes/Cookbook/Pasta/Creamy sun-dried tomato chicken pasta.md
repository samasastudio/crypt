# Creamy sun-dried tomato chicken pasta

**Pasta** | Page 90 | Serves 4 | 405 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 405 | 19.6g | 42.0g | 15.4g |

## Ingredients

- 250g dried pasta
- 1 tbs olive oil
- 1 red onion, diced
- 3 tbs tomato paste onion and sauté for 2
- 1 cup light
- 1/2 cup mozzarella, grated
- 1/2 cup reserved pasta
- 1/2-1 tsp paprika pasta

## Notes

We use Philadelphia 60% Less Fat Light Cooking Cream, Perfect Italiano Grated Mozzarella Cheese and Vetta Smart Protein Pasta for these macros. To increase the protein you can add chicken or protein of choice.
