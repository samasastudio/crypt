# Blueberry protein pancakes

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 2 slices soft white bread Spread the jam over the bread
- 1-2 tbs jam
- 1 egg
- 40ml milk the egg, milk, protein powder
- 20g vanilla protein
- 1 tsp butter
- 1 tbs granulated sweetener medium

## Notes

We use Natvia Reduced Sugar Jam, Macro Mike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein, Nuttelex Lite, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk and Natvia Natural Sweetener for these macros. Jam can be substituted for your favourite spread. We recommend trying it with Nutella and Biscoff too.
