# Caramelised banana protein pancakes

**Breakfasts** | Page 19 | Serves 2 | 243 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 243 | 13.5g | 34.9g | 4.5g |

## Ingredients

- 80g white spelt flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 cup vanilla protein
- 1 egg milk. Adjust the milk as needed
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tbs maple syrup (15ml)
- 1/2 cup milk (125ml)

## Notes

We use Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk and Macromike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder for these macros. We find vegan protein powder creates a softer pancake texture than whey protein. If using whey, you may need to reduce the milk slightly.
