# Apple & custard

**Breakfasts** | Page 9 | Serves 1 | 384 cal

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 40g oats
- 20g vanilla protein powder
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 small banana (40g)
- 100ml milk
- 1tbs maple syrup (15ml)
- 1-2 tbs thick custard
- 10g Biscoff spread
- 50g tinned apple, sliced

## Notes

You can use tinned or fresh apple slices. You can omit the Biscoff spread of replace it with almond butter or caramel sauce. We use Red Tractor Creamy Protein Oats, Macro Mike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein powder, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup and Pauls Premium Custard for these macros.
