# Creamy avocado toast

**Breakfasts** | Page 31 | Serves 2 | 339 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 339 | 15.2g | 37.9g | 12.9g |

## Ingredients

- 1/2 medium avocado (60g)
- 2 tbs light cream cheese
- 2 eggs, boiled
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 4 slices sourdough

## Notes

We use Stone Baked white sourdough and Philadelphia 40% Less Fat Light Spreadable Cream Cheese for these macros.
