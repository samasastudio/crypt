# Banana bread baked oats

**Breakfasts** | Page 13 | Serves 1 | 334 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 334 | 20.6g | 38.2g | 9.5g |

## Ingredients

- 1/2 banana (50g)
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/2 oats (40g)
- 20g vanilla protein powder (approx. 13 cm), reserving 1 tbs
- 1 tbs maple syrup (15ml)
- 3/4 tsp baking powder granulated sweetener and cinnamon
- 10-15g Biscoff spread

## Notes

We use Red Tractor Creamy Protein Oats, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Truvia sweetener and Macromike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder for these macros.
