# Apple & custard baked oats

## Ingredients

- 1/2 ripe banana (50g)
- 1/2 cup oats (40g)
- 20g salted caramel/
- 3/4 tsp baking powder
- 1 tbs maple syrup (15ml)
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/3 banana, sliced (30g)
- 10g chocolate, chopped

## Notes

We use Red Tractor Creamy Protein Oats, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, Lindt No Sugar Milk Chocolate, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Truvia sweetener and Macromike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder for these macros.
