# Biscoff donut baked oats

**Breakfasts** | Page 15 | Serves 1 | 343 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 343 | 21.0g | 41.4g | 10.2g |

## Ingredients

- 1/2 banana (50g)
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/2 oats (40g)
- 20g vanilla protein powder (approx. 13 cm), reserving 1 tbs
- 1 tbs maple syrup (15ml)
- 3/4 tsp baking powder granulated sweetener and cinnamon
- 10-15g chocolate

## Notes

We use Red Tractor Creamy Protein Oats, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Lindt No Sugar Milk Chocolate, Truvia Sweetener and Macromike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder for these macros.
