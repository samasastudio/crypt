# The best scrambled eggs

## Ingredients

- 2 tbs pesto (30-40g)
- 2 eggs
- 2 slices bread/ sourdough
- 1 tbs light ricotta/ cream

## Notes

You can use basil or sun-dried tomato pesto. We use Leggos Sun-Dried Tomato Pesto, Coles Light Cream Cheese and Country Split Bread for these macros.
