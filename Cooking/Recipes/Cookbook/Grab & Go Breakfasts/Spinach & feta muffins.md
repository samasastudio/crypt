# Spinach & feta muffins

**Grab & Go Breakfasts** | Page 52 | Serves 12 | 153 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 153 | 6.2g | 19.8g | 5.3g |

## Ingredients

- 400g pumpkin, peeled
- 1 egg Place pumpkin cubes on the baking tray
- 1 cup milk
- 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt
- 1/3 cup basil pesto (100g) together
- 2 cups plain flour (260g)
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 100g spinach, chopped
- 150g feta, crumbled skewer comes out clean
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
- 1-2 tbs pine nuts, optional

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, The Healthy Baker Flour, Leggo’s Traditional Basil Pesto and Danish feta for these macros. Muffins can be frozen for meal prep.
