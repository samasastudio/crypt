## Notes

We use Yopro Vanilla Yoghurt (for the filling), Macromike Choc Caramel Bar Protein Powder, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, Lindt No Sugar Milk Chocolate, Red Tractor Creamy Protein Oats and Cocobella Vanilla Coconut Yoghurt (for the ganache) for these macros. We recommend using coconut yoghurt for the ganache, as it will be a creamier texture, however Greek style yoghurt will also work. Please note you will not need the milk in the ganache if using coconut yoghurt.
