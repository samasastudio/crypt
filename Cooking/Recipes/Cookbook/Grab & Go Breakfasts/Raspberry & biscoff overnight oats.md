## Ingredients

- 1/4 yoghurt (60g) Slice strawberries
- 2 tbs maple syrup (30ml)
- 40g light cream cheese
- 1 cup oats (80g)
- 30g vanilla protein powder
- 1 tbs chia seeds (10g)

- 100g + sliced strawberries
- 1 lotus biscuit, optional decorate with

## Notes

We use Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, YoPro Vanilla Yoghurt, Red Tractor Protein Creamy Oats, Macromike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder and Vitawerx White Chocolate for these macros. Feel free to swap the strawberries out for another fruit of choice.
