# Pumpkin & pesto muffins

|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 2 wraps
- 3 eggs
- 75g rindless short cut bowl
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/4 cup light cheese, for 15-20

## Notes

We use Bega 50% Less Fat Cheese, Mountain Bread Rye Wraps and Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt for these macros. For a vegetarian version, omit the bacon and add veggies of choice.
