## Ingredients

- 2 Weet-Bix Crush Weet-Bix
- 80ml milk
- 1 tbs sweetener to form the base
- 1 shot of espresso
- 130g yoghurt
- 30g light cream cheese refridgerate for a
- 15g vanilla protein powder
- 1/2 - 1 tbs sweetener
- 1-2 tsp chocolate protein/

## Notes

We use Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Yopro Vanilla Yoghurt, Philadelphia 40% Less Fat Light Spreadable Cream Cheese, Macro Mike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup and Avalanche Sugar Free Drinking Chocolate for these macros. BASE METHOD 2 Weet-Bix Crush Weet-Bix in a bowl, then mix in the coffee, sweetener and 80ml milk water. Smooth over with a spoon 1 tbs sweetener to form the base. 1 shot of espresso In a separate bowl, mix together yoghurt, cream cheese, protein powder and sweetener. Spread CREAM over the base. 130g yoghurt Dust with chocolate powder and 30g light cream cheese refridgerate for a minimum of 1 hour or overnight. 15g vanilla protein powder 1/2 - 1 tbs sweetener, optional CHOCOLATE 1-2 tsp chocolate protein/ cacao powder Nutrition SERVINGS 1 SIZE 1 CALORIES 325 CARBS 33.6G FAT 7G PROTEIN 27.3G 44
