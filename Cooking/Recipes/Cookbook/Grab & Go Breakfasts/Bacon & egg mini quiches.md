# Bacon & egg mini quiches

**Grab & Go Breakfasts** | Page 56 | Serves 6 | 335 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 335 | 24.5g | 22.8g | 15.7g |

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs Lay the bacon on a lined baking
- 100g Greek yoghurt/
- 180°C for 10-15
- 6 English muffins
- 6 slices cheese

## Notes

We use Tiptop Wholemeal English Muffins, Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt and Coles Australian Smokey Individually Wrapped Slices for these macros. If using cottage cheese or ricotta, we like to blend it first for a smoother consistency, this is completely optional and won’t alter the taste.
