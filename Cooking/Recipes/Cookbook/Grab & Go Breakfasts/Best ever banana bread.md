## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt (60g)
- 1/4 cup olive oil (55g)
- 2 cups plain flour (260g) baking soda, salt and pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt into the prepared tin
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 200g baby spinach
- 150g feta, crumbled
- 2 tbs fresh herbs - dill

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, The Healthy Baker Flour and Danish feta for these macros. Muffins can be frozen for meal prep.
