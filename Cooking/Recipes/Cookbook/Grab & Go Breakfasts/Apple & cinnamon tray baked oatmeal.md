## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup oats (40g)
- 20g vanilla protein powder
- 1/2 tbs chia seeds
- 2 tbs yoghurt (30g)
- 1/2 cup unsweetened
- 50g raspberries/
- 10-15g Biscoff spread

## Notes

We use Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup, YoPro Vanilla Yoghurt, Red Tractor Protein Creamy Oats and Macromike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder for these macros.
