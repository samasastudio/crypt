# Mars bar inspired overnight oats

**Grab & Go Breakfasts** | Page 42 | Serves 1 | 328 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 328 | 28.4g | 34.4g | 7.5g |

## Ingredients

- 2 Weet-Bix Crush Weet-bix
- 80-100ml milk
- 1 tbs maple syrup the base
- 160g yoghurt
- 15g protein powder
- 10g Biscoff, melted
- 10g yoghurt/ milk

## Notes

Optional to sprinkle over 1/2 crushed lotus biscuit or top with some fruit (we like to add 1/2 sliced banana for a Banoffee vibe). To make this recipe gluten free, use gluten free Weet-Bix and substitute Biscoff spread for almond/ peanut butter. We use Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Yopro Salted Caramel Yoghurt, Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup and Macro Mike Salted Caramel Almond Protein Powder for these macros. BASE METHOD 2 Weet-Bix Crush Weet-bix in a bowl, then mix in the milk and maple syrup. 80-100ml milk Smooth over with a spoon to form 1 tbs maple syrup the base. In a separate bowl, mix the CREAM yoghurt and protein powder together until smooth. Spread 160g yoghurt over the base. 15g protein powder Mix biscoff and yoghurt/milk together until smooth. Optional to add a dash of milk/ water to thin. CARAMEL Spread caramel over the top of 10g Biscoff, melted the cream layer. 10g yoghurt/ milk Place in the fridge for a minimum of 1 hour or overnight.
