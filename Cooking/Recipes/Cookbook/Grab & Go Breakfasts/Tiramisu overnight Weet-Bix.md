# Tiramisu overnight Weet-Bix

**Grab & Go Breakfasts** | Page 46 | Serves 1 | 353 cal

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 2 Weet-Bix For the jam
- 80ml milk
- 1 tbs sweetener
- 130g yoghurt
- 30g light cream cheese Smooth over with a spoon to form
- 15g vanilla protein powder
- 1 tsp sweetener, optional
- 1 tsp lemon juice/zest, to smooth. Spread over the base
- 100g strawberries, sliced Nutrition
- 1/2 tbs sweetener
- 1/2 tsp chia seeds

## Notes

We use Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almond Milk, Yopro Vanilla Yoghurt, Philadelphia 40% Less Fat Light Spreadable Cream Cheese, Macro Mike Vanilla Buttercream Almond Protein Powder and Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup for these macros. Feel free to swap the strawberries out for another fruit of choice. BASE METHOD 2 Weet-Bix For the jam, add 50g strawberries and sweetener to a saucepan or 80ml milk microwave safe bowl, heat until 1 tbs sweetener softened. Mash with a fork, then sprinkle over the chia seeds and set aside to cool. CREAM Crush Weet-Bix in a bowl, then 130g yoghurt mix in the milk and sweetener. 30g light cream cheese Smooth over with a spoon to form the base. Top with remaining 15g vanilla protein powder, strawberries. optional In a separate bowl, mix cream 1 tsp sweetener, optional filling ingredients together until 1 tsp lemon juice/zest, to smooth. Spread over the base. taste Spread the jam on top and place in the fridge for a minimum of 1 hour or overnight.
