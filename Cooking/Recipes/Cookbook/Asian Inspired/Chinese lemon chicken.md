# Chinese lemon chicken

**Asian Inspired** | Page 168 | Serves 1 | 473 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 473 | 36.8g | 39.7g | 17.9g |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 125g salmon fillet drizzle over the tamari and maple
- 1 tbs tamari/ soy sauce syrup
- 1 tbs maple syrup/ honey
- 1 sprig spring onion, diced
- 1 tsp light kewpie mayo/ Top with the
- 1 nori sheet, optional salmon

## Notes

We use Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup and Kewpie Mayonnaise 50% Reduced Fat for these macros. Salmon can be substituted with tuna, chicken or tofu.
