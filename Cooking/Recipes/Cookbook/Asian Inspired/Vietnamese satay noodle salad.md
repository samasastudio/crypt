## Ingredients

- 2-3 cups vegetables of

## Notes

We use Wokka Noodles Singapore Style Shelf Fresh, Ayam Coconut Milk Light and Steeves Maples Sugar Free Maple Syrup for these macros. Our favourite plant-based chicken alternatives are Get Plant’d Vegan Roast Chicken or Plant Asia Plant-Based Roast Chicken.
