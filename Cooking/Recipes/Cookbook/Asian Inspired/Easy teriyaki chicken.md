# Easy teriyaki chicken

**Asian Inspired** | Page 172 | Serves 4 | 459 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 459 | 35.9g | 33.3g | 20.6g |

## Ingredients

- 1 tsp olive oil
- 500g chicken breast, diced
- 1 red capsicum, diced 5
- 1 bunch broccolini
- 400-500g
- 200ml canned light coconut
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 2 tbs sweet chilli sauce (35g)
- 1-2 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 1/2 - 3/4 cup chicken stock

## Notes

We use Ayam Coconut Milk Light, Ayam Thai Sweet Chilli Sauce Light and Mayver’s Smooth Natural Peanut Butter for these macros. For a vegetarian option, substitute the chicken with plant-based chicken or tofu. Add more or less veggies of your choice.
