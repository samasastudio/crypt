# Teriyaki salmon sushi bowl

**Asian Inspired** | Page 170 | Serves 2 | 432 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 432 | 41.4g | 47.4g | 7.5g |

## Ingredients

- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 300g chicken breast
- 250g cooked rice pan over medium
- 1 tsp sesame seeds, chicken
- 1 sprig spring onion, finely
- 1/4 cup tamari/ soy sauce caramelised
- 1/4 cup
- 1/2 - 1 tbs honey/ maple and slice
- 1 tbs Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 1 tsp sesame oil, optional

## Notes

We like to pair this with seamed green vegetables. For a vegetarian or vegan option, substitute the chicken with salmon or tofu. If you prefer a thicker sauce, combine 1 tsp of corn starch with a splash of water until it forms a smooth paste and mix into the sauce.
