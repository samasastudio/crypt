## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast, diced
- 1 egg, whisked
- 1/4 cup corn flour
- 1/4 tsp each salt & pepper salt and pepper
- 1-2 tbs olive oil, for frying
- 3 cups
- 1 tsp sesame seeds over medium
- 1/3 cup lemon juice
- 2 tbs honey
- 2 tbs granulated brown
- 1-2 tsp garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup chicken stock
- 1 tbs corn flour

## Notes

For a vegetarian or vegan option, replace the chicken with tofu or plant-based chicken. Optional to omit the egg from the batter.
