# Bang bang salmon

**Asian Inspired** | Page 176 | Serves 4 | 301 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 301 | 45.3g | 12.1g | 10.8g |

## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 medium carrots, sliced
- 150g snow peas, cut into thirds
- 1 red capsicum, sliced
- 20g lemongrass paste
- 25g ginger paste
- 3/4 tsp rice wine vinegar
- 2 tbs soy sauce
- 1 tbs brown sugar
- 1 cup canned coconut milk and brown sugar.
- 550g chicken breast, diced

## Notes

We use Ayam Coconut Milk Light for these macros. Nutritional information is for the stir-fry only, serve with rice of choice. For a vegetarian option, we recommend substituting the chicken with 2 packets of Malaysian tofu.
