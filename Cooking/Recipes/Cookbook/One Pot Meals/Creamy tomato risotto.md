# Creamy tomato risotto

**One Pot Meals** | Page 142 | Serves 4 | 443 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 443 | 34.0g | 43.8g | 14.2g |

## Ingredients

- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 500g chicken and pepper
- 1/2 tsp paprika medium-high
- 1 tbs unsalted butter
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced (or
- 1 tsp powder) medium-high
- 1 cup orzo pasta/ risoni
- 1 medium zucchini, grated for an additional 2
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 cup light
- 1/3 cup mozzarella, grated
- 30g feta, crumbled

## Notes

We use Philadelphia 60% Less Fat Light Cooking Cream, Perfect Italiano Grated Mozzarella Cheese, Riverina Fetta Greek Style and Barilla Risoni for these macros.
