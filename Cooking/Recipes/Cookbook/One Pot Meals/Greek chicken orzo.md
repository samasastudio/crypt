# Greek chicken orzo

**One Pot Meals** | Page 148 | Serves 5 | 437 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 437 | 35.3g | 37.4g | 16.3g |

## Ingredients

- 1 tbs olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, minced medium
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 carrot, grated
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 500g lean beef mince
- 500g tomato passata
- 1 can diced tomatoes (400g)
- 6 lasagna sheets (150g)
- 1/2 tsp each garlic powder
- 1/2 cup mozzarella, grated (50g)
- 1/4 cup parmesan, grated (25g)

## Notes

We use Vetta Smart Fibre Instant Lasagna Sheets, Perfect Italiano Grated Mozzarella Cheese and Parmesan for these macros. Optional to add béchamel sauce for extra yums (not included in the macros).
