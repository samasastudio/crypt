# Pulled Mexican chicken

**One Pot Meals** | Page 132 | Serves 4 | 422 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 422 | 44.4g | 40.5g | 6.9g |

## Ingredients

- 1 tbs olive oil
- 1 brown onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1-2 tbs Mexican seasoning
- 1-2 tbs tomato paste (25g) for 30 seconds
- 800g canned diced
- 125g canned sweet corn
- 3/4 cup vegetable/
- 600g chicken breast
- 120g rice, uncooked
- 1 cup slaw/ lettuce
- 30g cheese, grated

## Notes

We use Bega 50% Less Fat cheese and Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt for these macros. The Mexican chicken can also be used in tacos, burritos or nachos.
