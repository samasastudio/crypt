# Marry me chicken orzo

**One Pot Meals** | Page 146 | Serves 4 | 456 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 456 | 41.4g | 46.8g | 13.9g |

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced lemon zest, salt & pepper
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tbsp lemon juice medium high
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 zucchini, grated
- 1 red capsicum, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1 2/3 cups chicken stock
- 1 cup canned crushed
- 1 tbsp tomato
- 1 cup orzo/risoni (200g)
- 80g feta, crumbled

## Notes

We use Coles Danish Style Feta and Barilla Risoni for these macros. You can roast the tomatoes in the oven/air fryer for 10 minutes and add them at the end or blister them in the pan with the other vegetables.
