# Garlic butter rice

**Essentials & Sauces** | Page 237 | Serves 4 | 194 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 194 | 4.5g | 39.6g | 2.2g |

## Ingredients

- 1 cup basmati rice, Melt butter
- 1 1/2 cups water (375ml) fragrant
- 1 tbs butter (15g)
- 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 tsp salt

## Notes

We use Sunrice White Basmati Rice and Lite Nuttelex for these macros. Rinsing the rice is not required for this recipe.
