# Homemade flatbread

## Ingredients

- 150g self-raising flour
- 125g Greek yoghurt the dough onto a floured surface
- Pinch of salt
- 1-2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1-2 tbs butter, melted (20g)
- 1 tbs fresh parsley/ of baking paper or lightly floured

## Notes

We use The Healthy Baker Flour, Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt and Nuttelex Lite for theses macros. Self-raising flour can be replaced with an equal amount of plain flour plus 1 tsp baking powder. If you find the consistency of the dough is too sticky, please refer to the notes of our homemade flatbread recipe.
