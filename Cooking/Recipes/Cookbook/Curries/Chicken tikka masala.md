## Ingredients

- 270ml canned coconut
- 4-6 tbs green curry
- 500g chicken breast/ tofu
- 125-180ml chicken/ sweetener, fish sauce and lime leaves
- 4 kaffir lime leaves, torn
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 2 cups snow peas chicken is
- 1 red chilli, optional
- 150g rice, uncooked
- 1 lime, cut into wedges
- 1 sprig spring onion, diced

## Notes

We use Ayam Coconut Milk Light, Maesri Green Curry Paste and Natvia Brown Sweetener for theses macros. You can use coconut sugar, brown sugar, white sugar or maple syrup to sweeten the curry and adjust to your taste preference. SPICE MIX METHOD 270ml canned coconut Add 3 tbs of coconut milk to a saucepan milk over medium heat, simmer for 1 minute until 4-6 tbs green curry paste thickened. Add the curry paste and cook for 1-2 (112g) minutes. 500g chicken breast/ tofu, diced Add the remaining coconut milk, stock, 125-180ml chicken/ sweetener, fish sauce and lime leaves. Stir and vegetable stock simmer for 2 minutes. ½ tbs sweetener Add the chicken, reduce the heat to low and ½ tbs fish sauce simmer for 7 minutes. 4 kaffir lime leaves, torn Add the zucchini and simmer for another 5 1 zucchini, sliced minutes until vegetables have softened and 2 cups snow peas chicken is cooked through, 1 red chilli, optional Add snow peas, chilli and Thai basil leaves, ¼ cup Thai basil leaves simmer for another 2 minutes. 150g rice, uncooked Serve with rice, a squeeze of lime juice and 1 lime, cut into wedges garnish with spring onion. 1 sprig spring onion, diced Nutrition SERVINGS 4 SIZE 1 CALORIES 448 CARBS 42.5G FAT 13.8G PROTEIN 35.6G 157
