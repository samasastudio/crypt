## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, Bulla Light Thickened Cooking Cream and Natvia Natural Sweetener for these macros. MARINATE METHOD 600g chicken breast Place chicken in a bowl with 1/4 cup Greek yogurt (60g) remaining marinate ingredients, 1 tsp each - garam masala, toss to coat. Refrigerate for turmeric, paprika, fresh minimum 1 hour or overnight. ginger & fresh garlic Add the onion to a pan over medium heat, sauté for 2-3 CURRY minutes until softened. Add 1 onion, diced spices, almonds and tomatoes, 1 tsp each - turmeric & cook for 1 minute. Transfer the fresh garlic mixture to a blender and blitz 1/2 tsp each - garam masala, cumin, paprika until it forms a paste, set aside. 1/2 cup canned tomatoes To the same pan, cook the (150g) chicken for 5 minutes until 20g almonds/ cashews browned. Add the paste and cook 150ml chicken stock for an additional 2 minutes. 80ml light cooking cream Add the stock and simmer for 1 tbs sweetener 15 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and sauce has 1 tsp salt thickened. Stir in the cream, 150g rice, uncooked sweetener and salt, simmer until heated through. Nutrition Serve with rice. SERVINGS 4 SIZE 1 CALORIES 403 CARBS 35.9G FAT 8.3G PROTEIN 54.2G 153
