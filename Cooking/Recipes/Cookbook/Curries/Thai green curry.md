## Ingredients

- 1 tbs olive oil
- 1 broccoli, chopped
- 1 red capsicum, sliced
- 1 onion, diced browned
- 2 carrots, sliced into half
- 2 tsp minced garlic
- 4 tbs Thai red curry
- 380ml canned coconut milk
- 1 tbs brown sugar/ sweetener
- 1 tbs tamari/ soy sauce
- 2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
- 1 vegetable stock cube
- 150g rice, uncooked
- 1 –2 tbs cottage cheese/

## Notes

We use Ayam Coconut Milk Light, Ayam Thai Red Curry Paste, Natvia Brown Sweetener and chicken breast for theses macros.
