## Ingredients

- 2 tsp each - paprika
- 1 tsp each - turmeric
- 1/4 tsp chilli powder, with yoghurt, oil, 1/2 the spice
- 600g chicken breast, diced Melt the butter
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt medium
- 1/2 tbs each – butter & oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbs ginger, minced
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 400g canned diced
- 150ml light
- 150g rice, uncooked

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, Bulla Light Thickened Cooking Cream and Nuttelex Lite for these macros. SPICE MIX METHOD 2 tsp each - paprika, Mix the spice ingredients together garam masala, cumin in a small bowl. 1 tsp each - turmeric, coriander, salt Place the chicken in a large bowl 1/4 tsp chilli powder, with yoghurt, oil, 1/2 the spice optional mix, 1/2 the garlic and 1/2 the ginger, mix to coat. Refrigerate for minimum 1 hour or overnight. CURRY 600g chicken breast, diced Melt the butter in a pan over 1/2 cup Greek yogurt medium heat. Add the onion (120g) and sauté for 1-2 minutes. Add 1/2 tbs each – butter & oil remaining spice mix, garlic and ginger, cook for 30 seconds. Add 2 garlic cloves, minced tomatoes and cream, stir and 1 tbs ginger, minced simmer for 5 minutes. Transfer to 1/2 onion, diced a blender and blitz until smooth, 400g canned diced set aside. tomatoes/ passata To the same pan, add the chicken 150ml light cooking cream and cook for 5 minutes until 150g rice, uncooked browned. Add the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes until Nutrition chicken is cooked through. Serve with rice. SERVINGS 4 SIZE 1 CALORIES 465 CARBS 40.9G FAT 12.1G PROTEIN 54.8G 155
