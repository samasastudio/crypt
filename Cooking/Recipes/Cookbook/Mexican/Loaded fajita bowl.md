# Loaded fajita bowl

**Mexican** | Page 204 | Serves 1 | 420 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 420 | 43.2g | 27.2g | 14.5g |

## Ingredients

- 1-2 wraps
- 125g extra lean beef mince
- 2 tsp taco seasoning
- 1/3 cup can diced tomatoes/ Mexican
- 25g cheese, grated breaking it up with a spatula
- 2 tbs Greek yoghurt/ light sour Once the wraps are toasted
- 1/2 tomato, diced 3-5

## Notes

We use 1.5 Mountain Bread Rye Wraps, Bega 50% Less Fat cheese and Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt for these macros.
