# Sweet potato burrito bowl

**Mexican** | Page 202 | Serves 4 | 525 cal

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 525 | 44.3g | 42.4g | 21.9g |

## Ingredients

- 4 small sweet potatoes, diced into
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 500g extra lean beef mince
- 1 packet of taco seasoning (30g) Meanwhile
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 2 capsicums (1 red + 1 yellow), sliced
- 1 brown onion, sliced
- 1 tsp brown sugar browned
- 80g light cheese, grated 5
- 4 tbs light sour cream or Greek

## Notes

We use Old El Paso Reduced Salt Spice Mix Taco and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros. You can use white potato or sweet potato, to make it lower carb use Spud Lite Potatoes.
