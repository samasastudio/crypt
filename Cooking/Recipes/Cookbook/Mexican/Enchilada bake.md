# Enchilada bake

**Mexican** | Page 196 | Serves 4 | 469 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 469 | 47.7g | 21.9g | 17.2g |

## Ingredients

- 1 tbs olive oil
- 400g chicken breast, diced
- 1 onion, diced garlic, onion powder, salt & pepper
- 1 tsp each cumin & paprika
- 1 red capsicum, diced garlic
- 8 tortillas/ low carb tortillas
- 100g cheese, grated
- 1 bunch spring onion, diced
- 1 bunch coriander, finely

## Notes

We use Bega 50% Less Fat cheese and Mission Low Carb Wraps for these macros. You can use 4-8 small to medium or 8 larger tortillas. For a vegetarian version, substitute the chicken with beans of choice or plant based chicken.
