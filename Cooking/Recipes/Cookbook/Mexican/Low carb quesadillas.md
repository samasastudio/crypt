# Low carb quesadillas

**Mexican** | Page 198 | Serves 3 | 415 cal

## Ingredients

- 2 potatoes, diced
- 1 red onion, sliced into wedges
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 400g can chickpeas
- 70g tomato
- 1 cup vegetable stock
- 10-15g fajita seasoning
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 125g can sweet corn
- 1 tomato, diced 3 bowls. Top with the corn salsa, tomato
- 30g cheese, grated
- 3 tbs sour cream/ ricotta

## Notes

You can use white potato or sweet potato for this recipe. We use Spud Lite Potatoes and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros. CHICKPEA SALSA METHOD 2 potatoes, diced Preheat the oven to 200°C. 1 red onion, sliced into wedges Add potatoes and onion to an oven safe dish. 2 tsp olive oil Drizzle with 1/2 the olive oil and season with 400g can chickpeas, drained & salt & pepper. Place in the oven for 25 minutes rinsed or until crispy. 70g tomato paste Meanwhile, add the corn to a fry pan 1 cup vegetable stock over high heat and cook for 2-3 minutes 10-15g fajita seasoning until charred. Transfer to a bowl, add the 1 garlic clove, minced cucumber, lemon juice and season with salt & pepper. Mix to combine and set aside. To the same pan, heat remaining oil then add CORN SALSA the chickpeas, tomato paste, fajita seasoning 125g can sweet corn, drained and garlic. Mix and sauté for 1-2 minutes. 1/2 cucumber, diced Add the stock and simmer for another 5 Lemon/ lime juice, to taste minutes. Add the roasted potatoes to the pan, mix to combine then remove from the heat and cover with a lid. Rest for 1-2 minutes. TO SERVE 300g cooked rice Divide the rice and chickpea mixture between 1 tomato, diced 3 bowls. Top with the corn salsa, tomato and a tablespoon of sour cream.
