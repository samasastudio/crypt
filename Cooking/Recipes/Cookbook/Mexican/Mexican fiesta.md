## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast, diced (or 325g
- 1/2 onion, diced
- 1 capsicum, diced
- 400g canned kidney beans
- 1/2 cup corn kernals tomato passata and stock
- 1/2 cup cheese, grated (50g)
- 4-6 wraps/ tortillas
- 1 tbs each - paprika, cumin, oregano chicken and
- 2 tsp each - onion powder, garlic
- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 1 tbs plain flour
- 1 1/2 cups tomato passata Repeat with
- 1 cup chicken/ vegetable stock

## Notes

We use Bega 50% Less Fat cheese and Old El Paso Regular Tortillas for these macros. Kidney beans can be replaced with four bean mix, chickpeas, black beans or left out completely. For a vegetarian version, replace the chicken with Plant Based Chicken or an additional can of beans . If you are using rotisserie chicken, you can skip the cooking time of the chicken. Optional to use store bought enchilada sauce for convenience.
