# Mexican soul bowl

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 525 | 44.3g | 42.4g | 21.9g |

## Ingredients

- 2 large sweet potatoes
- 2 tsp olive oil taco seasoning
- 1 packet taco seasoning (30g)
- 3 tomatoes, diced
- 1/3 cup red onion, diced
- 1 small jalapeño, diced & seeds
- 1/2 cup coriander, finely chopped
- 1-2 tbs lime or lemon juice
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- 2 cups lettuce, chopped
- 80g light cheese, grated
- 4 tbs light sour cream or Greek

## Notes

We use Old El Paso Reduced Salt Spice Mix Taco and Coles Light Mozzarella cheese for these macros. You can use white potato or sweet potato, to make it lower carb use Spud Lite Potatoes. For extra yum, add a drizzle of Nando’s Perinaise sauce.
