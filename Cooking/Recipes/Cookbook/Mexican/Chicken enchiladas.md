# Chicken enchiladas

**Mexican** | Page 194 | Serves 4 | 474 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 474 | 45.5g | 35.2g | 17.0g |

## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast, diced (or 325g
- 1 1/3 cup light cheese, grated (120g)
- 6 flour tortillas
- 500-700ml Enchilada sauce, divided
- 1/2 tbs olive oil
- 1/2 onion, diced through
- 1 capsicum, thinly diced
- 1/3 cup light cream cheese (80g)

## Notes

We use Bega 50% Less Fat cheese, 6 x Old El Paso Mini Tortillas and Old El Paso Enchilada sauce for these macros. For a vegetarian version, replace the chicken with Plant Based Chicken. If you are using rotisserie chicken, you can skip the cooking time of the chicken. Optional to make your own enchilada sauce (refer to our Chicken Enchilada recipe).
