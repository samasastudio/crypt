# Sweet potato falafels

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 367 | 26.6g | 44.1g | 10.3g |

## Ingredients

- 2 x 185g can tuna in spring
- 1 egg or
- 1/2 cup onion, diced
- 1/2 cup fresh parsley or
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 cup breadcrumbs (120g)
- 1/2 tsp each garlic & onion
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- 2-3 tbsp extra virgin olive (approx 1.5cm thick)

## Notes

We recommend serving with our homemade sweet chilli mayo. Patties can be frozen for meal prep.
