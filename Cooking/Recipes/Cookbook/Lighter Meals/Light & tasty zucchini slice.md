# Light & tasty zucchini slice

**Lighter Meals** | Page 59 | Serves 9 | 86 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 86 | 6.6g | 7.9g | 2.9g |

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 80g self-raising flour
- 1/2 brown onion, diced
- 1 zucchini, grated & excess
- 100g ham/ bacon, diced
- 40g cheese, grated
- 1 tsp salt

## Notes

We use The Healthy Baker flour and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros.
