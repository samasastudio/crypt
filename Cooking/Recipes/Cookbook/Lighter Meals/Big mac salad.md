# Big mac salad

**Lighter Meals** | Page 77 | Serves 2 | 422 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 422 | 36.7g | 41.7g | 12.4g |

## Ingredients

- 250g chicken breast thick
- 2 tbs Greek yoghurt (30g) pepper
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1/4 tsp each paprika,
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard instructions
- 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbs parmesan, finely grated (15g)
- 1/2 anchovy fillet, finely chopped
- Salt & black pepper, to taste
- 100g dried pasta
- 1 egg (optional)
- 2-3 cups cos lettuce

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt and San Remo Penne Pasta for these macros.
