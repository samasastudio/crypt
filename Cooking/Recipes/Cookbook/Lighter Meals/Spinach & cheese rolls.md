# Spinach & cheese rolls

**Lighter Meals** | Page 71 | Serves 2 | 430 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 430 | 37.7g | 37.6g | 13.4g |

## Ingredients

- 250g chicken breast, diced Coat chicken with peri-peri
- 2 tsp peri-peri seasoning
- 1-2 cups lettuce, chopped for 7-10
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 red capsicum, diced water at a time
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- 1/4 cup sweet corn
- 1/2 cup black beans, chicken and dressing. Toss to
- 1 cup tortilla/ corn chips
- 1/2 avocado (50g)
- 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt
- 1/4 cup fresh coriander
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt & pepper, to taste

## Notes

We use Tostitos Cantina Tortilla Corn Chips and Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt for these macros.
