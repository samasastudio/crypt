# Veggie pakoras

**Lighter Meals** | Page 69 | Serves 15 | 56 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 56 | 2.1g | 4.7g | 4.1g |

## Ingredients

- 250g frozen spinach
- 1 egg Thaw
- 1/3 cup spring onion
- 250g low fat cottage
- 75g light cheese, grated
- 1 clove garlic, optional tightly
- 3 sheets pastry
- 1-2 tbs milk
- 1/2 tbs sesame seeds

## Notes

We use Art of Pastry Frozen Fillo Pastry Sheets and Coles light tasty cheese for these macros.
