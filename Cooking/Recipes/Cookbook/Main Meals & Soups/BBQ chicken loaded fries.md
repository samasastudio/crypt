## Ingredients

- 500g turkey mince
- 1 egg together
- 50g feta, crumbled
- 1/3 cup breadcrumbs (35g)
- 1/4 cup onion, diced
- 1/2 tsp each garlic powder
- 3 tbs fresh parsley
- 200g Greek yoghurt
- 1 tbs mayo or olive oil
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp zest
- 1 garlic clove, grated
- 1 cup basmati rice
- 1 tsp turmeric

## Notes

We use Lemnos smooth Fetta, Coles Australian Wheat Bread Crumbs and Chobani Natural Greek Yogurt for these macros.
