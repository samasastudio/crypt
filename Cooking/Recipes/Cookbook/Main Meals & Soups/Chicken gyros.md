## Ingredients

- 6-8 medium potatoes
- 1 tbs olive oil
- 1 small carrot, grated
- 2 garlic cloves, finely a fork
- 1 brown onion, diced soften
- 400g canned butter beans
- 1 tsp each cumin &
- 50g tomato
- 3/4 cup vegetable stock
- pepper.
- 1 1/2 cups slaw
- 3 tbs light sour cream
- 1 tbs parsley

## Notes

If you don’t have a microwave, skip step 2 and increase the baking time for the potatoes to a total of 35 minutes. For a healthier homemade slaw dressing, mix 1 tsp each of Dijon mustard, olive oil and vinegar. You can use white potato or sweet potato for this recipe. We use Spud Lite Potatoes and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros.
