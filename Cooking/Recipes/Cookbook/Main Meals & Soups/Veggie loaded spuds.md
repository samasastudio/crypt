# Veggie loaded spuds

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 426 | 34.7g | 46.3g | 9.7g |

## Ingredients

- 250g potato, thinly sliced
- 100g chicken breast Slice the potatoes into fries
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp each - garlic
- 2 tbs barbecue sauce
- 1 cup baby spinach
- 1/2 tomato, diced fry for an additional 10
- 1 tbs fresh parsley, golden and crispy
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 20g cheese, grated ingredients together
- 1/2 tbs light mayo

## Notes

We use Bega 50% Less Fat cheese, Bull’s-eye Original BBQ Sauce and Praise 99% Fat Free Mayonnaise for these macros.
