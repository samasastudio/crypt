## Ingredients

- 100g frozen chips
- 2 tbs tzatziki (30g)
- 1 tomato, sliced
- 1 cup lettuce, chopped Coat the chicken
- 2 small Lebanese wraps/
- 250g chicken breast Meanwhile
- 1/2 tbs olive oil non-stick fry pan for 10
- 1 tbs Greek yoghurt
- 1-2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbs lemon juice
- 1 tsp white wine vinegar
- 1/4 tsp salt chips

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, Black Swan Tzatziki Dip Skinny, Coles Frozen Straight Cut Potato Chips and Nana Bakery Traditional Lebanese Bread White for these macros. Chicken breast can be substituted with chicken thigh, lamb or beef.
