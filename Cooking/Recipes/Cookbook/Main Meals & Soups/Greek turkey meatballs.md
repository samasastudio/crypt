# Greek turkey meatballs

## Ingredients

- 4 soft hotdog rolls
- 500g extra lean beef mince with salt and pepper, mix
- 1 tsp each – salt, Italian
- 4 slices cheese
- 2 tbs creamy ranch
- 1 cup mixed lettuce

## Notes

We use Coles soft hot dog rolls, Coles Light Mozzarella cheese and Praise Ranch Dressing for these macros.
