# McChicken

**Burgers** | Page 187 | Serves 6 | 345 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 345 | 27.3g | 31.5g | 12.3g |

## Ingredients

- 400g turkey breast mince
- 1 egg ingredients except the oil together
- 2 small carrots, grated
- 1 tbs Dijon mustard into six patties
- 1 tsp garlic
- 1 tsp each onion powder & over medium
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley
- 1-2 tbs olive oil
- 6 x burger buns
- 6 x cheese slices
- 2 cups lettuce, shredded
- 2 tomatoes, sliced
- 1/2 onion, sliced
- 3-6 tbs sauce of choice

## Notes

We use Aldi Brioche Buns and Bega 50% Less Fat cheese for these macros. We recommend serving with our homemade sweet chilli mayo (not included in the macros). To make the burgers extra yum, add 1/4 cup of grated cheese into the patty mixture.
