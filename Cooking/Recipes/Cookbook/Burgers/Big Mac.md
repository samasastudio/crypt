## Ingredients

- 500g extra lean beef mince
- 4 burger cheese slices/ pepper, mould into 8 balls (2
- 4 burger buns
- 2 burger thins, halved -
- 1 cup lettuce, shredded
- 1/4 cup pickles
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise (60g)
- 1 tbs ketchup/ tomato
- 1 tbs mustard
- 1 tbs pickles, finely diced
- 1 tbs pickle juice patty, pickles and close with the
- 1/4 tsp each garlic powder, top bun lid

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Yoghurt, Praise 99% Fat Free Mayonnaise, Aldi Brioche Buns, Tiptop Burger Thins, French’s Classic Yellow American Mustard, Masterfoods Reduced Salt & Sugar Tomato Sauce and Dairylea Burger Cheese Slices for these macros.
