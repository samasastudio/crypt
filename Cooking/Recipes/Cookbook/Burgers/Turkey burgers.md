## Ingredients

- 2 burger buns
- 250g chicken breast, sliced medium-high
- 1/2 tsp dried mixed/
- 50g brie cheese, sliced on both sides
- 1 tomato, sliced
- 2-3 tbs cranberry sauce
- 1-2 tbs herbed mayo (20g)

## Notes

We use Aldi Brioche Buns, Ocean Spray Fruit Sauce Cranberry Jellied and Birch & Waite Free Range Seafood Sauce Parsley Dill & Tarragon for these macros.
