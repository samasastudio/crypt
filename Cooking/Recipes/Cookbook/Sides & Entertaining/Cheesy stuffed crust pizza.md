# Cheesy stuffed crust pizza

**Sides & Entertaining** | Page 227 | Serves 4 | 207 cal

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 1 tbs butter Melt the butter
- 3 cups frozen corn
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup coriander
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- 1 cup spring onion, sliced
- pepper to taste.
- 1/4 cup sour cream/ chips or as a side salad
- 2 tbs mayonnaise (30g)
- 20g parmesan, finely
- 2 tbs lime juice
- 1/4 tsp each paprika & salt

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, Praise Whole Egg Mayonnaise Light and Danish feta for these macros.
