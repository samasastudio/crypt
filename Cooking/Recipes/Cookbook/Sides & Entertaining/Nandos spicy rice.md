# Nandos spicy rice

**Sides & Entertaining** | Page 209 | Serves 4 | 165 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 165 | 8.6g | 6.8g | 11.4g |

## Ingredients

- 180g haloumi
- 2 tbs honey (30g) shallow bowl
- 1 tbs lemon juice together
- 1 tsp fresh rosemary/ over the haloumi. Marinate for 15

## Notes

We use Lemnos Salt Reduced Haloumi for these macros.
