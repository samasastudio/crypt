## Ingredients

- 70g self-raising flour
- 80g plain thick yoghurt
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 20g Babybel cheese/
- 2 tbs tomato paste (30g) the dough into a circle
- 30g+ mozzarella, grated To create the stuffed crust

## Notes

We use Chobani 0% Greek Yoghurt, The Healthy Baker Flour, Coles Light Mozzarella and Babybel Mini Cheese for these macros.
