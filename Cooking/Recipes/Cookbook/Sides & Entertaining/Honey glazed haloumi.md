# Honey glazed haloumi

**Sides & Entertaining** | Page 211 | Serves 24 | 49 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 49 | 5.6g | 3.1g | 1.5g |

## Ingredients

- 500g extra lean beef mince
- 1/2 onion, finely diced
- 1/2 carrot, grated
- 1/2 zucchini, grated
- 1-2 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 tbs tomato paste/ bbq sauce thoroughly with a fork
- 1 tsp dried thyme/ mixed herbs
- 1 tbs fresh parsley, chopped/ 1 tsp
- 1/4 cup dried breadcrumbs
- 1 egg, whisked
- 1/2 tbs salt
- 4-5 sheets mountain bread sprinkle over the sesame seeds
- 2 tbs sesame seeds, optional

## Notes

We use 5 sheets of Mountain Bread Rye Wraps for these macros. You can use puff pastry or your preferred wrap, including gluten free.
