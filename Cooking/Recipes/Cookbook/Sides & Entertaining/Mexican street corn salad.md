# Mexican street corn salad

**Sides & Entertaining** | Page 229 | Serves 12 | 60 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 60 | 2.9g | 4.9g | 2.8g |

## Ingredients

- 600g potatoes, peeled &
- 1 tbs garlic butter, melted
- 1 tbs mild taco seasoning
- 80g parmesan cheese
- 2-3 tbs smokey chipotle
- 1-2 tsp fresh chives, finely

## Notes

We use Spud Light Potatoes and Perfect Italiano Parmesan Cheese for these macros. We use Masterfoods Smokey Chipotle sauce to make these stacks extra yum (not included in the macros).
