# Air fryer carrot chips

|----------|---------|-------|-----|

## Ingredients

- 500g potatoes, thinly Slice the potatoes into thin strips
- 1 tbs corn/ rice flour
- 1 tsp salt 200°C
- salt and seasoning. Toss to evenly

## Notes

You can use white potato or sweet potato for this recipe. We use Spud Lite Potatoes for these macros.
