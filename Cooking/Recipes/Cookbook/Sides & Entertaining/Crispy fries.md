# Crispy fries

**Sides & Entertaining** | Page 223 | Serves 6 | 165 cal

## Nutrition

| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|----------|---------|-------|-----|
| 165 | 8.0g | 16.4g | 7.8g |

## Ingredients

- 2 zucchinis
- 200°C
- 2 eggs
- 1/3 cup plain flour (45g) and pat dry with paper towel, set
- 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1 tbs dried herbs
- 1 tsp salt bowl. Beat the eggs
- 1/4 cup parmesan, grated
- 1 tsp olive oil (5ml) baking tray or into the air fryer
- 2 tbs herbed mayo (30g)

## Notes

For the dried herbs, we recommend 1 tsp each rosemary, oregano, basil and thyme. We use the Birch & Waite Free Range Seafood Sauce Parsley Dill & Tarragon for these macros.
